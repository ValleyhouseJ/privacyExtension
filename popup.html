    What this means

    • Cookie: A small note the site saves in your browser so it can recognize you.
    • Session: Disappears when you close the browser.
    • Persistent: Stays after you close the browser until its expiry date.
    • Flags:
      • Secure: Browser sends it only over HTTPS, not over unsecured HTTP.
      • HttpOnly: Page scripts cannot read it. Only the browser can send it back to the site.
      • SameSite=lax: Sent when you visit the site directly, but usually not on cross-site requests.
      • SameSite=strict: Sent only when you stay on the same site.
      • SameSite=none: Sent in all cases, but only allowed together with Secure.
      • SameSite=unspecified: Modern browsers treat it like Lax.
